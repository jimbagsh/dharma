My suggestion to my Mongolian friends, is to look back, and find what was truthful, what was deep inside of the heart of who you are as a people, as individuals. That is part of that mystery, and that is where you will find the happiness that will lead you into a future that will be unique. It won't be fake American, or fake Chinese, it will be uniquely Mongolian. I can honestly say from my own experience, through the Buddhist path, you will find that.
